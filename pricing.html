Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lineup Card really free for Lineup DJ users?
Yes. If you have an active Lineup DJ subscription, you can log into Lineup Card with the same email and use the Free tier at no extra cost. Your roster, players, and position preferences sync automatically.
What do I get with the Pro plan?
Pro unlocks unlimited teams, the advanced auto-assign algorithm, the strategy slider for competitive play weighting, full game history and team statistics, lineup sharing with coaches and parents, and priority support.
How does the 7-day free trial work?
When you start the Pro trial, you get full access to every Pro feature for 7 days at no charge. If you decide it's not for you, cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. You can cancel at any time from your account settings. You'll keep access to Pro features through the end of your current billing period.
What's the difference between Basic and Advanced auto-assign?
The Basic algorithm assigns positions fairly based on player preferences. The Advanced algorithm adds strategy weighting, so you can prioritize competitive play alongside fairness using the strategy slider.
Do I need Lineup DJ to use Lineup Card?
No. Anyone can sign up for the Pro plan directly. The Free tier is a perk for existing Lineup DJ subscribers, but Lineup Card works as a standalone tool on the Pro plan.
